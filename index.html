👋 Welcome to Murchison Falls!

One of the most beautiful National Parks in Africa and the World overall!
This place hosts Africa's most powerful waterfall. A haven for mammals, birds and reptiles

The falls is widely recognized as the world's most powerful waterfall, formed by the Victoria Nile River.
The landmark lends its name to the surrounding Murchison Falls National Park, which stands as the oldest and largest protected wildlife conservation


The park is the home to a varied species to name a few

  1. Lions
  2. Hippos
  3. Elephants
  4. Giraffes
  5. Crocodiles
  6. Shoebill Stork

Plan Your Adventure!
Breath taking and life changing activities include:
  • Nile Boat Curise. A mandatory three-hour boat safari sails smoothly upriver to the roiling basin directly underneath the falls, providing front-row viewing of riverbanks packed with wildlife
  • Game Drive. Track lions, leopards, and buffalo across the sweeping open savanna north of the Nile
  • Falls Hike. A 45-minute trek starting near the boat drop-off point up to the edge of the gorge to feel the dramatic mist
  • Birding excursion. Home to 450+ bird species including the rare shoebill stork, African skimmer, Goliath heron, and Abyssinian roller
  • Nile Fishing. The river teems with enormous Nile perch
  • Chimp Trek. Combine Murchison with chimp trekking in the ancient Budongo Forest, home to over 800 habituated chimpanzees and one of East Africa's largest remaining mahogany forests
